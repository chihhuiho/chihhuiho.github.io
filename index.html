About

I am a Senior Applied AI Researcher at Articul8, where I build domain-specific multimodal GenAI systems. I received my PhD from the University of California San Diego (UCSD) in the Statistical Visual Computing Lab, advised by Prof. Nuno Vasconcelos. My research interests include multimodal GenAI, computer vision, and deep learning. I have also interned at MERL and Amazon AWS AI. Before my PhD, I earned my MS in CSE from UCSD and my BS from National Chiao Tung University in Taiwan.
