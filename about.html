Background

I received my PhD from the University of California San Diego (UCSD) in the Statistical Visual Computing Lab (SVCL), where I was advised by Prof. Nuno Vasconcelos.

My research interests include GenAI, computer vision, and deep learning. I was a research intern at Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories (MERL) and Amazon AWS AI. Before joining SVCL, I received my master's degree in UCSD CSE and my bachelor's degree from National Chiao Tung University in Taiwan.

Before joining UCSD, I worked as a research assistant in the Computer Vision Research Center at NCTU. I was also an exchange student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign during my junior year and a research intern at Cornell University.
